COVERED is a UK social enterprise that installs smart dispensers giving out free, plastic-free period products. The service is funded entirely by brands advertising on the dispensers' 32-inch screens — users pay nothing, venues host for free, and advertisers fund the products. The first pilot launches in Exeter.
Really free — no coins, no cards, no subscriptions. Advertisers pay for the ad space on our screens and packaging, and that revenue covers the products, the machines and the restocking. The only "catch" is a short sponsor message while the machine dispenses.
High-quality, plastic-free and biodegradable pads and tampons, dispensed in convenient 4-packs. Good for you, better for the planet.
Anyone who needs period products — students, commuters, visitors, anyone caught off guard. A gentle monthly fair-use limit keeps stock available for everyone, with a simple way to request more if you need it.
We're building to UK GDPR and PECR standards from day one — privacy by design. Marketing consent is always a separate, un-ticked opt-in, and you keep full control of your data, including the right to erasure. Our initial pilot collects no personal data at all.
Brand-safe only. Think beauty, wellness, fashion, food and student services — never adult, gambling, political or otherwise sensitive content. Venues approve the environment their visitors experience.
If you run a venue, host a unit. If you work with a brand, tell your marketing team about us. And if you just like the idea — follow the journey when our channels launch, and spread the word.
