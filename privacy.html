Privacy Notice

COVERED (“Covered by Mills”) · Last updated 6 July 2026

COVERED provides free period products from dispensers funded by advertising. To keep the service free and fairly shared, we ask you to verify a mobile number before a product is dispensed. This notice explains exactly what we do — and don’t do — with your information.

The short version:
  • We never store your phone number. It is converted to an irreversible code (a “hash”) used only to apply the fair-use limit of 4 products per month.
  • Verification texts are sent by our SMS provider; we don’t see or keep the number afterwards.
  • No account, no name, no payment details — ever.
  • Marketing texts happen only if you tick the optional box, and you can withdraw any time.
  • Usage records are automatically deleted on a schedule.

Who we are

COVERED is a UK social enterprise providing free period products. Data protection contact: ameliaireland18@gmail.com.

What we collect and why

DataWhyLegal basis (UK GDPR)
Your mobile number (moment of use only) To text you a one-time verification code that proves you’re a real person and prevents stock abuse. The number itself is not stored by us — only a one-way hash. Legitimate interests — protecting a limited free resource with the least intrusive workable check.
A one-way hash of your number, plus a monthly counter To apply the fair-use limit (4 products per month per person). The hash cannot be turned back into your number. Legitimate interests.
Session and usage events (scan time, machine, ad viewed, dispense) To operate the machines, keep them stocked, and report anonymous totals to the venues and advertisers who fund the free products. Legitimate interests.
Marketing consent flag + timestamp (optional) Only if you tick the optional box: to text you offers and news from COVERED. Entirely separate from getting your product. Consent (and PECR). Withdraw any time.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data.
  • We never store your raw phone number in our database.
  • We never profile you or track you across other websites.
  • We never make receiving a product conditional on marketing consent.

Who processes data for us

  • Twilio — delivers the one-time verification text (sees the number to send the SMS, under its data-processing agreement).
  • Supabase — hosts our database (hashed identifiers and usage records).
  • n8n — runs the workflow that connects the pieces (EU-hosted).

How long we keep things

  • Verification attempt logs: 24 hours.
  • Session and usage events: 90 days, then deleted automatically every night.
  • The hashed identifier and monthly counter: kept while the service operates, solely to enforce the monthly limit.
  • Marketing consent record: until you withdraw it or ask us to erase you.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, erase, or export the data linked to your number, to withdraw marketing consent, or to object to our processing. Email ameliaireland18@gmail.com from a message that includes the mobile number you used (we need it to compute the hash and find your records — we can’t look you up any other way). We respond within one month.

If you’re unhappy with our answer you can complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office at ico.org.uk.

Changes

We’ll update this page if the service changes, and material changes will be signposted on the machines and in the app.
