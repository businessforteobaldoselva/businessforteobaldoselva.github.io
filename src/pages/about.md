      COVERED began with a stubborn question: why, in 2026, is anyone still caught without a pad or tampon when they need one?

      More than one in ten UK women and people who menstruate struggled to afford period products in the last year. We think the fix isn't charity boxes or coin slots — it's a business model where someone else happily pays. Brands want real attention in real places. We have dispensers in exactly those places. The maths does the rest.
